Classic Homemade Waffles

This classic waffle recipe from Allrecipes is a simple, traditional breakfast dish designed to produce light, fluffy waffles with a crisp, golden exterior. The batter is made by combining basic dry ingredients like flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt, then mixing them with wet ingredients such as eggs, warm milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. The two mixtures are blended together to form a smooth batter.

Once prepared, the batter is poured into a preheated waffle iron and cooked until the waffles are golden and crisp on the outside. The recipe is quick to make, taking about 25 minutes in total, and yields several waffles that are best served immediately, often with toppings like butter or syrup. Overall, it's a straightforward, reliable recipe that focuses on classic flavors and texture, making it ideal for everyday breakfasts or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • teaspoon salt or to taste

  • 4 teaspoons baking powder

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

  • 2 large eggs

  • 1 ½ cups warm milk

  • ⅓ cup butter, melted

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.

  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.

  5. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.

  6. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.

  7. Serve immediately and enjoy

Creamy pesto shrimp

This creamy pesto shrimp recipe is a rich, comforting pasta dish built around a simple but indulgent sauce. It starts with cooked linguine, which is later coated in a velvety mixture made by melting butter and simmering heavy cream, then blending in grated Parmesan cheese and basil pesto. The sauce thickens into a smooth, flavorful base with a balance of creamy, cheesy, and herby notes.

Shrimp are added directly into the sauce and cooked just until they turn pink and tender, absorbing the flavors as they finish cooking. The final dish is assembled by tossing or serving the shrimp and sauce over the hot pasta, creating a hearty meal that comes together fairly quickly but tastes rich and restaurant-worthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound linguine pasta

  • ½ cup butter

  • 2 cups heavy cream

  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • ⅓ cup pesto

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients together

  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in linguine and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes; drain.

  3. Stir Parmesan cheese into cream sauce until thoroughly mixed. Stir in pesto and cook until thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.

  4. Stir in shrimp and cook until they turn pink, about 5 minutes. Serve sauce over hot linguine.

  5. Serve hot and enjoy !

Ploughman's Sandwich

The Ploughman's Sandwich is a quick, rustic sandwich inspired by the traditional British “ploughman's lunch,” combining simple, hearty ingredients into a satisfying meal. It layers slices of smoked ham and sharp white cheddar cheese on crusty bread, along with fresh elements like tomato and peppery arugula. Tangy additions such as sweet gherkin pickles and wholegrain mustard bring contrast and depth, while a light spread of butter ties everything together.

The method is straightforward and requires no cooking. You simply assemble the sandwich by spreading butter and mustard on the bread, then stacking the ham, cheese, vegetables, and pickles before closing it up. The result is a balanced mix of savory, fresh, and tangy flavors, with a satisfying combination of textures from crisp vegetables, soft bread, and firm cheese.

settings/pages

Ingredients

  • 2 slices crusty bread

  • 1 teaspoon butter, softened

  • handful of arugula

  • 2 sweet gherkin pickles, sliced

  • 3 slices smoked ham

  • 2 slices white Cheddar cheese

  • 1 small tomato, sliced

  • 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard

Steps

  1. Lightly butter both slices of bread.

  2. Layer the arugula, pickles, ham, Cheddar, and tomatoes on one slice of buttered bread.

  3. Spread the mustard on the remaining buttered bread slice, and place on top. Cut sandwich in half, and serve.
